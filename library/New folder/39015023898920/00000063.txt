It is convenient to divide the computation into a number of steps.
Step 1. Preparation of the experimental and treatment allocation plans. The experimental plan, Table
16, shows the block number in the first column. In the second column opposite each block number (and
in the same row) are given the treatments appearing in this block, and below each treatment number is
shown the corresponding observed yield. The third column of the experimental plan shows the block totals,
B₁, obtained by adding the yields of all the plots in the block, (j). The last column of the field plan gives
the block average, Bj/k, obtained by dividing the corresponding item in the previous column by k. It should
be noted that each subcolumn of column (2) gives a complete replication, and the totals of the yields in
these subcolumns given in the last row of Table 16 are replication totals. The total of the third column gives
the grand total, G, of all the observations. Finally below the last column is entered the grand mean, G/N,
for later use (N= vr=bk, being the total number of plots).
